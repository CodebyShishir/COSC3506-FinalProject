# SplitEase: User Documentation

Welcome to SplitEase! This free, straightforward web tool helps you track collective spendings amongst friends, roommates, and travel buddies. Follow these simple instructions to effortlessly manage your shared finances.

## Getting Started

### 1. Register and Login
- Browse to the `index.html` file or your live XAMPP server address (e.g., `http://localhost/SplitEase/index.html`).
- Click the **"Start Splitting!"** or **Register** button. 
- Input a valid Name, Email Address, and a secure password.
- Upon successful registration, you will be redirected to the Dashboard. Use your credentials to log in.

### 2. Dashboard Overview
Once logged in, the `dashboard.html` screen displays:
- A "Log Out" button positioned centrally or in the upper right.
- Your **Active Expense Groups**.
- Your overall balance profile (i.e., you are Owed $X, or you Owe $Y).

## Managing Expenses

### 3. Creating a Cost Bunch (Group)
If you are planning a new trip or managing apartment utilities:
1. Click the **"+ New Group"** button.
2. Enter a descriptive title (e.g., "Trip to Montreal").
3. Your list of groups will instantly update to include your new "Cost Bunch."

### 4. Logging an Expense
1. Select one of your Groups from the Dashboard.
2. Inside the Group feed, select **"Add Expense"**.
3. You will be prompted to enter:
    - **Description**: What did you buy? (e.g., "Dinner at Joe's").
    - **Total Amount**: Simply type the numerical amount (e.g., `100.50`).
4. Click submit. By default, the system recognizes *you* as the payer and divides the total evenly among all users associated with this Group.

### 5. Tracking Live Balances
- On your Group view, scroll down to the **"Balances"** section.
- SplitEase automatically offsets debts! It cross-references every transaction.
- Instead of seeing a cluttered timeline of 50 transactions, you simply see the *Final Outcome*: 
  - *"Jane owes John $45"*
  - *"Mark is settled up."*

### 6. Settling Debts
Has Jane handed John a $50 bill, settling her $45 debt?
1. John (the creditor) navigates to his Group Balances.
2. Next to Jane's debt, he clicks the **"Mark Settled/Paid"** button.
3. The ledger immediately updates their respective balances, leaving it clean and transparent!

Enjoy using SplitEase, and say goodbye to spreadsheet math!
